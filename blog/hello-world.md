Welcome to my blog! This is my first post—mainly here to kick things off and make sure everything is working.

## How this blog came to be

I’m currently experimenting with GitHub Copilot. I was never particularly good at design or making things look polished, so I decided to leave that part to Copilot and focus more on the coding.

To test things out, I built this small personal site with blog support. Right now, it’s fully static—but who knows, I might extend it with dynamic functionality in the future.

## What this blog is about

This is where I’ll write about things I find interesting—mostly programming and software architecture, with the occasional post about video games, movies, series, and music I enjoy.

No strict schedule, no clickbait titles. Just notes from someone who spends a bit too much time thinking about code.

## What to expect

- Deep dives into topics I’m learning or revisiting  
- Recommendations for learning resources (books, sites, tutorials, courses)  
- Suggestions, impressions, and ratings of pop culture
